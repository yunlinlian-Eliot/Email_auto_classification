## Data Dictionary: Email Extraction Output
This document provides a detailed explanation of the data fields generated by the EmailAutomation script.

**🔒 Data Privacy Note**
To ensure data privacy and confidentiality, all sensitive information in this sample output (including names, packages to extend and other metrics)
are replaced by anonymous information.

**🌙 Column Name,Data Type,Description**

Date,DateTime,The original timestamp when the email was received.
Sender,String,The name and email address of the sender.
Subject,String,"The subject line of the email, decoded for readability.+1"
Summary,String,A truncated snippet (first 150 characters) of the email body to provide a quick preview of the request.
Search_Command,String (Link),"Key Feature: A unique identifier derived from the Message-ID. This creates a clickable link or command 
to instantly locate the original email in the inbox, drastically minimizing manual searching time.+1"

**💡 Why "Search_Command" Matters**

In a "disaster" inbox filled with irrelevant noise (job applications, inquiries, etc.), finding a specific email can take minutes.
The Search_Command field:
Eliminates Manual Searching: Directly links the extracted data back to the source.
Enhances Verification: Allows the user to verify the full context of a "Service Extension" request with one click.
Operational Efficiency: This feature is a core component of the 90% efficiency gain achieved by this tool.
